About me

I am a first-year graduate student in ECE at Rutgers University advised by Jorge Ortiz. I plan to spend my doctoral studies exploring the intersections of Machine Learning, AI, and Human-Computer Interaction to find my path into making opportunities of developing skills in science and technology more accessible in less advanced countries by mobilizing the power of technology itself.

I completed my undergrad in ECE + CS at Rutgers through the Honors College. Prior to that I completed my post-secondary education in Bangladesh, where I was born and brought up.
