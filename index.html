Taqiya is a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Rutgers University, advised by Jorge Ortiz. Her research develops socially cognizant multimodal systems for smart environments, integrating Multimodal Machine Learning and Causal Reasoning. She works on developing methods that fuse behavioral, physiological, and environmental signals to understand human state and improve autonomous decision-making in intelligent spaces. Her broader goal is to advance human-centered AI/ML that supports well-being and sustainable interaction in everyday environments.

She completed her undergraduate studies in ECE and Computer Science at Rutgers through the Honors College. She completed her post-secondary education in Bangladesh, where she was born and raised.
