About me

I am a fourth-year undergraduate student pursuing Electrical & Computer Engineering and Computer Science at Rutgers University through the Honors College. I completed my post-secondary education in Bangladesh, where I was born and brought up.

After completing my Bachelors degree, I plan to pursue a PhD in Machine Learning and AI. I aspire to use my education and training to make opportunities of developing skills in science and technology more accessible to people living in less advanced countries of the world.
